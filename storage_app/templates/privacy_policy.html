Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password.

Storage Data: We store your uploaded files, file metadata, and folder structures.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our service, including upload/download patterns and feature usage.

Payment Information: For paid plans, we collect billing information through our secure payment processor.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our cloud storage services
  • To process your payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you important service notifications and updates
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All files are encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Transmission: Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular Backups: Regular data backups to prevent data loss

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (storage providers, payment processors)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • During business transfers (merger, acquisition, or sale)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can access and download your stored files at any time
  • Correction: You can update your account information
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: You can export your data from our platform
  • Objection: You can object to certain data processing activities

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Your personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Your stored files are permanently erased from our servers
  • Backup copies are retained for up to 60 days for disaster recovery purposes
