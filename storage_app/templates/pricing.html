Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments through our secure payment gateway.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Our free plan includes 5GB of storage forever. No credit card required to get started.

How secure is my data?

Your files are encrypted with AES-256 encryption and stored in secure data centers with 99.9% uptime.

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

You'll receive alerts and once you reach your limit, you have to upgrade your plan or free up space.

Can I share files with non-users?

Yes! All plans allow you to create shareable links that anyone can access, even without a CloudVTS account. You can also add password protection for extra security.

Can I access my files from multiple devices?

Yes! CloudVTS works on all devices - desktop, mobile, and tablet. Your files sync automatically across all your devices.

What file types are supported?

We support all file types - documents, images, videos, audio, archives, and more. Some file types can be previewed directly in your browser.
